üåü Medium-Term Strategies (3-12 Months)

  3. Business Travel Optimization: Review necessity of frequent travel. Implement rotation systems, virtual meeting alternatives, and travel allowance/recovery time policies.
  4. Predictive Retention System: Deploy the risk scoring model monthly to identify at-risk employees. Create automated alerts for managers when employees exceed risk thresholds.
  5. Job Satisfaction Monitoring: Implement quarterly pulse surveys focused on job satisfaction and work-life balance. Create rapid response teams to address emerging issues.
  6. Recognition & Rewards Program: Design non-monetary recognition systems for high performers in high-risk roles. Implement peer recognition platforms and quarterly excellence awards.
